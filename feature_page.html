Crafted Signature

Refreshing handcrafted beverages designed to balance rich flavors and smooth textures. From creamy lattes and mocha blends to unique foams and matcha creations, each drink is crafted to deliver a signature experience in every sip.

Caffeine

A classic collection of coffee favorites brewed to perfection. Enjoy bold espresso, smooth lattes, and creamy cappuccinos — available hot or iced. For coffee purists, we also offer expertly crafted manual brews like V60 and Chemex, as well as refreshing cold brew options for a slow and smooth caffeine fix.

Meals

A satisfying selection of rice meals featuring flavorful classics — from crispy chicken cutlets and savory pork sisig to hearty beef, fish, and sausages. Each meal is paired with steamed rice, perfect for a filling and delicious dining experience.

Snacks

A delightful variety of light bites and comfort food, perfect for any time of day. From hearty sandwiches like Patty Melt, Tuna Melt, and Ham & Cheese, to cheesy, flavor-packed quesadillas.
